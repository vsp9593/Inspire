ABOUT INSTITUTE

Government Polytechnic, Karad, is a premiere technical institute in western Maharashtra, established in 1957 by Government of Maharashtra, as per the visionary education policy of Late Yashawantraoji Chavan, first Chief Minister of Maharashtra. The prime objective of establishing this institute was to impart quality technical education to youth in rural part of Maharashtra.
Started with only one course, today G.P.Karad has grown accommodating 6 courses namely Civil Engineering, Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering, Instrumentation Engineering and Mechanical Engineering. Second shift is also started for programs Civil Engineering, Electronics And Telecommunication Engineering and Mechanical Engineering. About 1900 students are studying in this institute. Well-equipped laboratories, highly qualified faculty, ample space, resourceful library are strengths of the institute.
Various activities are being undertaken for development of the students and institute. AICTE funded Projects like MODROBs are implemented. Institute is also preparing to get accredited from NBA in next two years. MSBTE through its monitoring process, have awarded 'Excellent' grade to all courses.

    A Sate Level Event Organized By Department of Computer, Electrical and Instrumentation Engineering of GOVERNMENT POLYTECHNIC, KARAD

    Inspire'18 is a state level event organized by Government Polytechnic, Karad. The major objective of the event is dynamic development of students. It aims to improve presentation skills, reading habits, research abilities, confidence , technical writing, technical skills, self-writing ability, logical thinking, circuit designing and general knowledge.
    The Engineering fellows must be aware of modern techniques, latest trends & researches, standard procedures, etc. INspire'18 through its subcomponents like circuit building, C-War, technical paper / poster presentation and quiz provides a platform for students to express, innovate and explore the challenging engineering world.
