About Quizzaster Event

Fun, Challenge And Excitement

Quizzaster is Non-Technical event.This event helps to improve the general knowledge of students.A Quizzaster is an event which can also be called as 'Mind Sport'.Where in the players either as individuals or in teams attempt to answer question posted to them. Quizzaster includes Screening test,Buzzer Round and the Rapid Fire round. Inspire'18 use it as brief assessment or tool to measure the growth in the knowledge,abilities or skills of students either on a general level or restricted to specific field for instance Science,Maths etc.
